About this Marie:

I love teaming up with good people and going wild together. We work as artists, creative technologists, scientists and designers at the intersection of physical, human and digital experiences. We co-create immersive, interactive playgrounds and invite our audiences to participate in multi-sensory adventures, to move, listen, see, feel, learn, think, reflect, exchange, and to have fun.

Realization:

I co-created mixed-reality environments for audiovisual party nights, media art festivals, hackathons and tech conferences, for example a jungle installation, a dancing stage, and an empathizer. I also code generative systems that visualize music and motion. With every project I dive into new tools and processes – always with a hands-on attitude, an open mind and some geeky madness.

impact:

I help bringing inspiring ideas to life, all the way from concept development to stunning executions – on a wide spectrum of cutting-edge projects, ranging from film and motion, installations and interactive experiences, to experimental new formats.
